HOW WE WORK

  • Assessment

    In a series of interviews, our CTO Panel becomes familiar with your professional goals and assesses your current readiness to reach them.

  • Mentoring

    If necessary, we'll pair you with a CTO mentor who uses their personal playbook to guide you in developing your skill set towards reaching your goals.

  • Launch

    We'll help you set interviews with hiring managers in our network. You'll interview with confidence, already having the endorsement for that role from our CTOs.
